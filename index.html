Our Beginnings

Muntasebaat Hifz was started with the barakaat of the first Urs Mubarak of Sayedna Mohammed Burhanuddin (RA) with the vision to do a small khidmat in the destined fulfillment of Our Beloved Aqa Moula Sayedna Aaliqadr Mufaddal Saifuddin's (TUS) umeed 'har ghar ma ek Hafiz hoi'

Become a Haafiz !

If you are a muntasebaat and want to become a haafiz, you can enroll in the program by filling out this form. We are gradually accepting new applicants and will contact you as space becomes avalaible.

Join the Khidmat !

If you are a muntasebaat and a haafiz, and would love to join this khidmat, then please fill this form. Someone will get back to you soon.
